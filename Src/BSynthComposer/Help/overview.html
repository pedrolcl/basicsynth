BasicSynth Composer

BasicSynth Composer is a synthesizer and music scoring program based on the BasicSynth sound generation libraries. It includes project management, editors for Notelist files and instruments, interactive preview of instrument sounds through a virtual piano keyboard, mixer, and sound generation all in one program. To get started using BasicSynth Composer, follow the instructions found in the Step-by-step page.

Several example compositions are installed with the program. When you first open the program, the examples are available under the File->Recent Files menu. Open each project and then select Project->Generate to play the compositions.

This documentation assumes you already understand the basics of sound synthesis. For those who are just starting to learn synthesis techniques, the following books will be helpful. These are standard textbooks used in universities to teach sound synthesis. With a few exceptions, the examples in the books can be easily implemented using the BasicSynth modular synthesis instrument.

Computer Music: Synthesis, Composition, and Performance, Charles Dodge and Thomas A. Jerse

Computer Sound Design: Synthesis Techniques and Programming, Eduardo Miranda

The Computer Music Tutorial, Curtis Roads

In addition, the two volumes of Musimathics by Gareth Loy are extremely useful in uderstanding the mathmatical foundations of music and digital sound generation.

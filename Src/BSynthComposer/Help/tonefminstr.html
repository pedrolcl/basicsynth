Tone FM Instrument

The FMTone instrument is similar to the Tone instrument, but uses a two-oscillator FM generator in place of the single oscillator. The carrier-modulator ratio and modulation index are fixed for the duration of a note.

Patch Diagram

If a more complex FM algorithms or dynamic control over the index of modulation is required, use the FMSynth instrument, Matrix Synth instrument, or the Modular Synth instrument.

Tone Instrument

The Tone instrument contains a single oscillator, ADSR envelope generator, LFO generator and pitch bend. The waveform for the oscillator is selectable, providing for a variety of sounds. Although limited in abilty to produce a dyamic timbre, it is fast to execute and very easy to configure.
